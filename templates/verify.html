How Munchly Works

✻

Our objective is to prevent food waste by connecting restaurants/supermarkets with those in need. Acting as a web/mobile platform, we incentivize donation and cooperation from businesses with our revolutionary rewards programs.

Register

Users: Fill out the user registration form with the required information and provide a zip code where you will be picking up food at.

Restaurants: Fill out the restaurant registration form with the required information.

Vetting Process

The system will determine a user's eligibility in this program using financial information such as paystubs. If approved, you will have access to the resources. There is no vetting process for restaurants.


Profit

Users can take utilize Munchly to their heart's content as long as restaurants have food available. Businesses can partake in our reward system, where points are determined by the quantity in donations. Both sides win!
